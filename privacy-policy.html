01 Overview

Yumar Game ("we," "our," or "us") develops and publishes mobile games for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play our games.

By downloading or playing our games, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

02 Data We Collect

We collect data in two ways: automatically through the SDKs integrated in our games, and data you voluntarily provide.

Automatically Collected Data
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID, device model, OS version)
  • Gameplay events and progress (levels completed, session duration)
  • App performance data (crashes, errors, load times)
  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country/region)
  • In-app purchase history (processed by Google Play)
Data You Provide
  • Email address (only if you contact us directly)
  • Any information included in support requests or feedback

We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment card details. All in-app purchases are handled entirely by Google Play.

04 Advertising

Our games display advertisements powered by Google AdMob, AppLovin MAX, IronSource and Appodeal. These ad networks may use your Advertising ID to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

Your Ad Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
    Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

  • You can reset your Advertising ID at any time from the same menu
  • Opting out does not remove ads — you will still see non-personalized ads

  • Visit google.com/settings/ads to manage Google ad preferences

  • Visit applovin.com/optout to manage AppLovin ad preferences

  • Visit appodeal.com/sdk-opt-out/ to manage Appodeal ad preferences

05 Data Retention

We retain collected data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Game progress data is retained in Firebase Firestore as long as your account is active
  • Analytics data is retained according to Google's default retention policies (up to 14 months)
  • Support emails are retained for up to 2 years

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

06 Children's Privacy

Our games are rated for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will promptly delete such information from our systems.

07 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you

  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data

  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate data

  • Opt-out — opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 4)

  • Portability — request a portable copy of your data (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the email address in the Contact section. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase Security Rules to restrict unauthorized access to Firestore.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
